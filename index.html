About Better

Better is your personal AI relationship counselor, designed to help couples navigate the complexities of love, communication, and emotional connection. Our empathetic AI companion provides personalized guidance to strengthen your relationship.

Key Features:

  • Personalized AI counseling based on your unique situation
  • Safe and private conversations with complete confidentiality
  • Multiple counseling types for different relationship scenarios
  • Daily relationship tips and insights
  • Session history to track your progress
  • English language support

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Better work?

Better uses advanced AI technology to provide personalized relationship counseling. Simply choose your counseling type, start a conversation, and receive tailored advice based on your specific situation.

Is my privacy protected?

Yes, absolutely. Your conversations are completely private and stored locally on your device. We believe in the power of honest, open communication without compromising your personal information.

Can Better replace professional therapy?

Better is designed to complement, not replace, professional relationship counseling when needed. It's a helpful tool for daily relationship guidance and communication improvement.

What languages does Better support?

Better currently supports English language. More languages will be added in future updates.

How do I start a counseling session?

Open the app, tap "Start a new counseling session," choose the counseling type that best matches your situation, and begin your conversation with our AI counselor.

Can I view my past sessions?

Yes, you can access all your past counseling sessions through the "View past sessions" feature. This helps you track your progress and revisit important conversations.

How does the free usage work?

Better offers free daily usage: 2 counseling sessions and 10 conversations per day. After reaching these limits, you can watch ads to recharge - either 1 additional session or 5 more conversations per ad view.

How do I get more sessions and conversations?

When you reach your daily free limits, simply watch a short advertisement to recharge. Each ad view gives you either 1 additional counseling session or 5 more conversations. This helps us keep the app free while providing quality service.
